Welcome to FrameDrop

The fastest way for photographers to cull a shoot. Swipe through your photos like Tinder — keep the ones you love, toss the rest.

Fast swipe-based culling
AI quality analysis
Export kept photos as zip
Step 1

Load your shoot

Drag and drop your photos onto the drop zone, or click Browse files to pick them from your folder. You can load hundreds at once.

Supports JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC and RAW formats.
Step 2

Swipe to cull

Review each photo and decide instantly — keep or delete.

Delete Swipe left · Click Delete · Press ←
Keep Swipe right · Click Keep · Press →
Changed your mind? Press Z or tap the undo button anytime.
Practice Round

Try it yourself

Swipe or click the buttons below. We'll guide you through keep, delete, and undo.

Export your selects

Come back anytime
